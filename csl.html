Coffeecakes & Sweet Loaves

Prantl’s coffeecakes are made by hand in the German tradition with the same recipes we’ve been using for nearly half a century. Choose from: Maple Nut, Chewy Pecan, Almond, Caramel Nut, Raspberry Almond, Cherry, Apricot, Meltaway, Dutch Apple, Cinnamon Crumb, or the “Beehive”. We also have festive holiday coffeecakes that will put a smile on anyone’s face. Meanwhile, our delicious sweet loaves make a perfect addition to any breakfast, lunch, or tea spread. We offer a changing rotation of cake loaves every season and keep the favorites year round: Plain Pound Cake, Chocolate Chip Sour Cream, and our best-seller, Zesty Lemon.
