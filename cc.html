Cakes & Tortes

We are famous for our cakes, not to mention our burnt almond torte. The burnt almond torte available daily in our stores serves 8 – 9 people. We also make larger tortes to order. As for more traditional special occasion cakes, our most popular batter flavor is yellow, our most popular filling is vanilla pudding (a Prantl’s tradition) and our most popular icing is our white buttercream. But you don’t need to follow the crowd, we’ve got lots of other choices and the combinations are almost endless! We offer several 7” cakes (chocolate sour cream, marble, half and half, roses in the snow) in the store daily. We also take advance orders for special occasion cakes that serve anywhere from 6 – 72 people (including the torte!). And we make a few extra birthday cakes each day for our stores, just in case you forgot to call ahead!
